Everything you need to know about DevConf 2026

Who should attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf is built for developers, software engineers, founders, and tech enthusiasts who want to learn about the latest industry trends, network, and level up their skills.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes! Standard pass holders get access to digital recordings for 30 days, while Pro and Team pass holders get lifetime access to all session recordings.

Can I upgrade my ticket later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade from a Standard pass to a Pro pass at any time before the event starts by visiting your profile dashboard or contacting support.

Are there any team discounts?

Yes, our Team pass offers a massive discount for up to 10 people, which includes exclusive perks like dedicated lounge access and private workshop bookings.
